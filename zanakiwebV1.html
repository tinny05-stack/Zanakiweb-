Discover our rich history and commitment to educational excellence

Our History

Zanaki Secondary School is one of the oldest schools in Tanzania. It was established in 1939 by His Highness Aga Khan, and later taken over by the government in the 1970s, when its name was changed to Zanaki Secondary School.The school currently has 1,150 female students from Form One to Form Six, with 59 Tanzanian teachers and 3 non-teaching staff members.

Our Mission

Using the official curriculum, we are committed to provide our students with challenging programmes, conducive learning environment and opportunities for both personal and intellectual development so as to prepare them for concrete career studies and help them become independent lifelong learners.

Our Vision

To offer our students the opportunity to transform their visions into reality.

To increase the percentage of those who pass in Division I and Il by 70% and reduce the failure rate to less than 1% for O-level and 0% for A-level by year 2013.

To maintain our achievements in the other areas of School life.

Admission Process

Join our community of learners and achievers

Step-by-Step Admission Process

1

Application Submission

Complete and submit the admission application form along with required documents. Applications can be submitted online or in person at our admissions office.

2

Document Review

Our admissions team will review all submitted documents and verify the information provided. This process typically takes 3-5 business days.

3

Assessment Test

Students will take an age-appropriate assessment test to evaluate their current academic level and learning needs.

4

Interview

A brief interview with the student and parents/guardians to discuss expectations, goals, and school policies.

5

Admission Decision

Admission decision will be communicated within one week of completing all requirements. Successful applicants will receive enrollment instructions.

6

Enrollment

Complete the enrollment process by paying fees, submitting additional forms, and attending the orientation session.

Requirements for New Students

  • Completed admission application form
  • Birth certificate or passport copy
  • Previous school transcripts and report cards
  • Medical records and immunization certificates
  • Two recent passport-sized photographs
  • Parent/guardian identification documents
  • Proof of residence (utility bill or lease agreement)
  • Transfer certificate from previous school (if applicable)
